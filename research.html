Distractor · Research Wing · July 2026

The Museum of Possible Museums

An exhibition of open databases, hung as the sites they might one day become. Each label records what a collection is, how its data leaves the building, and on what terms — the three questions build.mjs asks of any dataset before turning it into a Distractor.

Press Space or R to be led to a random acquisition-ready exhibit  ·  ● open ● conditions apply ● not for reuse

I · The dumpBulk metadata — a CSV, JSON, or crawlable API — obtainable once, at build time, ideally without a key.
II · The pictureA direct, hotlinkable image URL (or IIIF pattern) that a browser may load from a static page.
III · The termsAn open license — CC0 or public domain preferred; attribution welcome; non-commercial noted on the label.

Room I

The American Wing

Where open access was invented as a bulk download. Four collections here can be rehung with little more than a find-and-replace.

Cleveland Museum of Art

Cleveland, Ohio · est. 1913

Medium
CSV & JSON on GitHub (Git LFS) — image URLs already inside the dump
Dimensions
~61,000 records; ~34,000 with images
Provenance
github.com/ClevelandMuseumArt/openaccess

CC0. The nearest thing to a find-and-replace on the MoMA build — first acquisition recommended.

Now on view — a working Distractor hangs here

Art Institute of Chicago

Chicago, Illinois

Medium
Monthly JSONL dump on S3; images conjured deterministically from image_id via IIIF at 843px
Dimensions
~120,000+ artworks
Provenance
github.com/art-institute-of-chicago/api-data

CC0-equivalent; filter is_public_domain. Seurat and Hopper, on demand.

Now on view — a working Distractor hangs here

Dimensions

Provenance

CC0, with Wikidata IDs thrown in. One join step at build time.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

New York, New York

Medium
CC0 CSV on GitHub — but the pictures live behind a per-object API call (primaryImage)
Dimensions
470,000+ records; ~200,000 public-domain images
Provenance
github.com/metmuseum/openaccess

CC0. Condition: requires a one-time enrichment crawl of several hundred thousand calls; commit the result.

Smithsonian Open Access

Washington, D.C. · nineteen museums at once

registry.opendata.aws/smithsonian-open-access

CC0. Condition: heterogeneous — heavy filtering to find the art among the stamps and specimens. See also Room V.

Room II

The European Galleries

The flagships kept their doors shut; the national galleries of Denmark and the city museums of Paris, Frankfurt, Berlin and Vienna opened theirs instead.

SMK — Statens Museum for Kunst

Copenhagen · National Gallery of Denmark

Medium
Keyless JSON API; whole collection crawlable in ~90 requests; direct image_native JPEGs plus IIIF
Dimensions
88,000+ works; ~40,000 with images
Provenance
api.smk.dk

CC0, flagged per record. Europe's Cleveland — the finest ergonomics east of the Atlantic.

Paris Musées Open Content

Paris · the fourteen museums of the city

Medium
GraphQL API, token free and self-service; HD image URLs in the response
Dimensions
~150,000 reproductions — Carnavalet, Petit Palais, Musée d'Art Moderne…
Provenance
apicollections.parismusees.paris.fr

CC0 for public-domain works. The anchor of French open access.

Städel Museum

Frankfurt am Main

Dimensions
22,000+ public-domain works with images
Provenance
sammlung.staedelmuseum.de/api/oai

Metadata CC0; images CC BY-SA, credit "Städel Museum". The cleanest German source.

Staatliche Museen zu Berlin

Berlin · nineteen collections

~270,000 records
Provenance
recherche.smb.museum

Wien Museum

Provenance

Amsterdam

~800,000 objects

Science Museum Group

London, York, Manchester, Bradford

Dimensions

DigiVatLib

Museo Egizio

Turin · Italy's one open-access museum

Medium
Harvest via its Wikimedia Commons category and Wikidata — keyless, bulk, hotlink-friendly
Dimensions
~3,000 objects; ~5,500 images on Commons
Provenance
collezioni.museoegizio.it

CC0. Winner of Italy's first "Museo Open Culture" award — because it is the outlier.

Finna.fi & the Nordic aggregators

Helsinki · Oslo · Stockholm

Medium
Finna: keyless REST, CC0 metadata, rights-filterable. DigitaltMuseum: 4M+ objects, clean image URLs, one free key. K-samsök: replaced end of 2026 — don't lean on it
Dimensions
Millions of records across Finland, Norway, Sweden
Provenance
api.finna.fi · api.dimu.org

Per-record licenses; filter to the CC0/PD subset. Nasjonalmuseet is reached through DigitaltMuseum.

Room III

Architecture

The institutions devoted to architecture mostly guard their drawings; the open material hides in libraries, city archives, and one Berlin university.

Architekturmuseum der TU Berlin

Berlin · drawings since the sixteenth century

Medium
CSV export of search results, LIDO XML per object, 3000px JPG downloads — no key
Dimensions
~163,000 catalogued; ~92,000 public-domain and downloadable
Provenance
architekturmuseum.ub.tu-berlin.de

Public Domain Mark. Schinkel's Berlin, sheet by sheet — the architecture counterpart to Cleveland.

Historic American Buildings Survey

Washington, D.C. · Library of Congress · HABS/HAER/HALS

Medium
Keyless JSON API, paged politely (~3s between requests); image_url arrays and IIIF on tile.loc.gov
Dimensions
~40,000+ surveyed sites; hundreds of thousands of photographs and measured drawings
Provenance
loc.gov/collections/…hhh

United States government documentation — public domain. The great American building record.

Sanborn Fire Insurance Maps

Provenance
data.labs.loc.gov/sanborn

Deutsche Fotothek

Room V

The Sculpture Court

Objects in the round. One collection makes a random-3D-object site as easy as the original — verified live, CORS headers and all.

Smithsonian Open Access 3D

Washington, D.C. · Digitization Program Office

Medium
Public S3 bucket, anonymously listable; Draco GLBs at three levels of detail (~1–2 MB), poster JPEGs, titles in scene.svx.json; Access-Control-Allow-Origin: * confirmed — loads straight into <model-viewer>
Dimensions
~2,360 3D packages — gunboats, gems, Lincoln's life mask
Provenance
3d.si.edu

CC0 (confirm per record at build time). No server, no proxy, no key — the whole MoMA pattern, in three dimensions.

Now on view — a working Distractor hangs here

Three D Scans

Oliver Laric · since 2012

Medium
STL/OBJ files, no API — convert to GLB and self-host
Dimensions
~150+ museum sculpture scans of unusual quality
Provenance
threedscans.com

No copyright restrictions — the cleanest license in the court. Condition: conversion labour.

Sketchfab, in memoriam

Absorbed into Epic's Fab, 2025–26

Condition report
CC0 and CC-SA licenses do not exist on Fab; affected models lose their downloads; the old download API is explicitly temporary

Do not build on a platform mid-demolition. The Smithsonian's own bucket outlives it.

Lower Level

The Vault

Collections you may visit but not take home. Their public-domain works escape through Wikimedia Commons; the institutions themselves are closed to this build.

Louvre · Orsay · Pompidou

Paris

Condition report
Per-object JSON but no enumeration; images restricted to private and educational use; Orsay offers nothing; Pompidou's century is still in copyright

Take the Commons route.

Prado · Uffizi · Vatican Museums

Madrid · Florence · Vatican City

Condition report
A commercial image bank; a browse-only archive under a fee-based reproduction regime; a catalogue still "in implementation," no API at all

Sophisticated inside, sealed outside.

British Museum · NPG · National Gallery

London

Condition report
No API or dump and CC BY-NC-SA; no programmatic access and low-res ND terms; CC0 data but NC-ND images on a deprecated server

Announced openings that never quite opened.

The architecture keepers

RIBA · CCA · TU München · Cité de l'architecture · Az W

Condition report
Rights-managed picture libraries, fee-based reproductions, or NC-ND with written-permission clauses

The discipline's own museums, closed to the discipline.

The estates

Le Corbusier · Frank Lloyd Wright · Aalto · Bauhaus-Archiv

Condition report
ADAGP rights until 2036; JSTOR-gated; paid research service; ~500 works online without licenses

Modernism remains privately held.

Fallen repositories

Carnegie · Tate · Cooper Hewitt · Harvard · Canmore

Condition report
A deleted repo; a dump frozen in 2014 without images; one frozen in 2017; terms forbidding caches older than two weeks; a service switched off in 2025

Open access is a practice, not a press release.
